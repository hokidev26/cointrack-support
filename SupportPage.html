Getting started

Add a record

Open CoinTrack, tap the add button, enter the amount, category, date, and notes, then save.

Use voice input

Speak naturally and review the generated result before saving. Clear speech in a quiet environment works best.

Review analytics

Use summaries and category charts to understand spending patterns and budget progress.

Manage data

Use in-app settings to manage export, account binding, sync, notifications, or deletion when available.

FAQ

Is my financial data stored on a server?

Financial records are stored locally on your device by default. If optional cloud sync is enabled, relevant data may be transmitted securely to support synchronization.

Can I edit a record after voice recognition?

Yes. Always review the recognized result before saving, and edit amount, category, date, or note if needed.

How do I remove my data?

You can delete local data through available in-app settings or by deleting the app from your device. For account or privacy requests, contact support by email.

Why do I see ads or AI usage limits?

If the app includes ads or AI features, these services may have usage limits or reward-based access. Please check the in-app descriptions for the current rules.

Does CoinTrack provide financial advice?

No. CoinTrack is for personal record keeping. Summaries, categories, and insights are informational only and are not financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.
