Frequently asked questions

Common questions about using CoinTrack.

How do I add an expense?

Open the app, tap the add button, enter the amount, category, date, and note if needed, then save the record.

How does voice input work?

Tap the voice input option and speak clearly, for example: “Lunch 150 today.” CoinTrack will help convert the sentence into a record. You can review and edit the result before saving.

Where is my financial data stored?

Your financial records are stored locally on your device by default. If cloud sync is enabled, related data may be transmitted securely to provide synchronization across your devices.

How can I export or delete my data?

Use the in-app settings if export or deletion options are available. You may also delete the app to remove local data from the device. For privacy requests, contact support by email.

Is CoinTrack financial advice?

No. CoinTrack is a personal recording and organization tool. Any summaries or insights are for reference only and should not be considered financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.
